# VLC Media Player

## Play/Pause

    SPACE

## Toggle Fullscreen

    F

## Play faster/slower

    +/-
    ]/[ for fine control)

## Reset playback speed

    =

## Next/previous track

    N/P

## Jump forward/back

    Shift+Right/Left   # 5 seconds
    Alt + Right/left   # 10 seconds
    Ctrl + Right/left   # 1 minute
    Ctrl + Alt + Right/left   # 5 minutes

## Volume up/down/mute

    Ctrl + Up/Down
    M (mute)

## Hide/show playlist

    Ctrl + L
